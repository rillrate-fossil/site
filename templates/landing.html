Features

Low-latency

We use state of the art technologies to bring you a surprisingly fast system for monitoring your apps in real-time.

Zero-configuration

Your app will be automatically configured to provide only requested streams of metrics and only requested streams will be transferred.

Multiple platforms

We offer the library for many popular programming languages like: Rust, Python, Node.js. And other languages will be supported soon.

Live controls

You can add controls to your apps and tune own parameters on the fly from the dashboard.

Real-time charts

Charts reflect actual state of your apps and never stucked even if data was accidentally delayed.

Powerful dashboard

On the dashboard you can render tables, charts, counters, histograms and more. All that items will be adjusted for the perfect view.
